What is speeqe?

Speeqe is group chat, simplified.

Talk to friends or co-workers in real time, or search past conversations to see what you missed.

Why use speeqe?

  • It's easy.
  • No signup required.
  • Chat right in your browser or with your favorite program.
  • Personalize with themes and vanity domains.

How does it work?

  1. Create a chat room by thinking of a name. For example: politics.

  2. Share the room's Web page with anyone. For the above example, the URL would be politics.speeqe.com.

  3. Start talking.
